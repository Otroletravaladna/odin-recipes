Chipa

Learn how to do this latin-american traditional and delicious dish in a few steps!

There's plenty ways to make the famous "Chipa" but the difference in thr recipe i'm showing is that this is gluten free. It is for this reason that we need to be extra careful with the treatment of the ingredients and the cross contamination. We are gonna need some almond flour and of course a lot of cheese!

Ingredients

  • Almond flour
  • Olive Oil
  • A lot of Fucking cheese
  • Water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Mix the almond flour with the flour
  2. Add the water and the olive oil and then form a dough
  3. Add the whole fucking cheese to the dough
  4. Make some balls with your hands and put'em in da oven
